SIMPLE CONFLICT-CHECK WORKFLOW

1. Obtain attorney name and law firm.
2. Obtain names of the patient, providers, hospitals, and imaging groups involved.
3. Check whether you have:
   - treated the patient
   - worked with any key defendant recently
   - current or prior contractual relationship with a party
   - internal peer review involvement in the matter
4. Confirm jurisdiction and approximate litigation stage.
5. Do NOT review substantive confidential records until the conflict screen is complete.
6. If no conflict, provide secure transfer instructions.
7. Confirm scope:
   - preliminary merit review
   - standard of care review
   - causation-related analysis
   - AI error / misdiagnosis review
   - report preparation
   - deposition support
